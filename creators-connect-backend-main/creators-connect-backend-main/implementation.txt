 Step 1 – Enable 2-Step Verification

Go to:
 https://myaccount.google.com/security

Turn ON:

2-Step Verification


Without this, App Password option will NOT appear.

 Step 2 – Generate App Password

After enabling 2FA:

Go to:
 https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

Select:

App → Mail

Device → Other (Custom)

Give name:

CreatorConnect


Click Generate

You will get something like:

abcd efgh ijkl mnop


Remove spaces:

abcdefghijklmnop


That is your email passkey.
